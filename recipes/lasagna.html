Lasagna

Description

This is a (fake) recipe for a standard meat lasagna. It uses a mixture of ground pork and beef. Vegetables beyond onions can be added. It is recommended to make your own sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of lasagna noodles
  • 10 oz of ricotta cheese
  • Yellow onion, chopped
  • 1/2 lb ground beef, 85% lean
  • 1/2 lb ground pork
  • 28 oz can whole, peeled San Marzano tomatoes
  • oregano
  • pepper
  • salt
  • olive oil
  • garlic

Instructions

  1. prep meat
    1. Heat olive oil in 12 inch skillet or dutch oven on medium to medium-high
    2. Once warm enough, add all meat, 1/4 of the chopped onion, 1 tsp salt, and 1/2 tsp black pepper
    3. Stir occasionally to break up meat and cook until browned
    4. Remove from pan and set aside. Reserve the pan
  2. Make sauce
    1. Using pan from meat cooking, add a little more oil
    2. Add 1/2 the chopped onion and soften (chopped carrot can be added too
    3. Drain tomatoes by pouring in a colander. Add tomatoes into heated pan
    4. Add more salt and pepper, and oregano
    5. Bring to simmer and cook until your desired thickness
    6. add the cooked meat at the end
  3. Assemble
    1. soak noodles in a pan for 15 minutes
    2. Once all above is done, start layering
    3. In 9in x 13in casserole dish, layer in the following order 3 times
      1. noodles
      2. ricotta, spread evenly across
      3. sauce
  4. Bake at 350 F for 45 minutes
  5. Let sit for 30 minutes after baking to allow it to set
