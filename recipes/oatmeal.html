Oatmeal

Description

Sometimes you want something simple and warm for breakfast. Oatmeal is the answer. It can be customized with any number of toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oatmeal (steel cut, old fashioned, thick cut preferred
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp (optional)
  • 1 egg (optional)

Making

  1. Add water and optional salt to saucepan and bring to a boil
  2. Stir in the oatmeal and reduce heat. Stir occasionally until most of the water is gone
  3. Add cracked egg, if adding, and stir until it's mixed in
  4. Turn off heat and let sit in pan or transfer to bowl for eating
  5. Add whatever toppings you desire, e.g. fruit, honey, nuts, peanut butter, chocolate chips, hemp hearts, yogurt, cocunut flakes, etc
